## About Me
Hi! This is Tingting Chen's home on the Web!

Tingting Chen is a PhD candidate at the National University of Singapore (NUS), working under the supervision of Dr. Dianbo Liu in the CogAI4Sci. Her research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biomedical science, with a focus on applying machine learning to ophthalmology and neurodegenerative diseases. She has also contributed to the development of Automatic Evolution and Discovery Engines that assist in hypothesis generation and experimental design.

Tingting’s work is driven by a strong commitment to scientific rigor and interdisciplinary innovation, aiming to bridge the gap between algorithmic intelligence and translational biomedical applications.
